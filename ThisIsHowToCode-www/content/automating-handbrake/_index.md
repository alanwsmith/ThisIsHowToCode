Presets
-------

Presets are done with `-Z` (Enclose names containing spaces in double quotation marks (e.g. "Preset Name"))

    HandBrakeCLI -Z "Very Fast 1080p30" -i "input.mkv" -o "output-2.mp4"


Handbrake things to try:

    (Constant Quality)
    -q 20
    
    (Encode)
    -e x264

    (Audio)
    -B 160

Presets:

    -Z. --preset <string>
